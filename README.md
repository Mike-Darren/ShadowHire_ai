## 📄 Using ShadowHire_ai to Evaluate Your Resume

ShadowHire_ai works by analyzing your uploaded resume and giving you feedback. Here’s how to use it:

1. Open the app and log in if needed.

2. Find the **Upload Resume** button, usually on the main screen.

3. Click it and select your resume file. Supported formats are `.pdf`, `.docx`, and `.txt`.

4. Wait a moment while ShadowHire_ai reads your document.

5. You will see three main reports:

   - **Hiring Probability Score:** A number showing how likely you are to get hired based on your resume.

   - **Skill Gap Analysis:** A list of skills you have and those you might need for your target jobs.

   - **Recruiter Psychology Simulation:** Insights on how a recruiter might view your resume’s strengths and weaknesses.
